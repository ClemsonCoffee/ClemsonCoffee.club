Welcome!

We are a club dedicated to everything about America's favorite beverage. Our goal is to bring together a diverse group of individuals who share a passion for specialty coffee.

Our Club Mission

Clemson Coffee Club plans to connect individuals to the their community using their shared interest in coffee.  We emphasize that the coffee community is not just local but a global network, from farm to cup. We plan to accomplish our mission in 3 steps.

Coffee Awareness

Coffee as a beverage has a mixed reputation. Some love it, others hate it. We believe that within the thousands of combinations of varietals, roast levels, and brew methods there is a cup of coffee for everyone. â€‹Our goal is to demonstrate how coffee is more than a unitary flavor, it is a taste experience that expresses the origin and decisions made from farm to cup.

Weekly Meetings

Clemson Coffee Club pledges to meet at least once a week during both the Fall and Spring academic semesters. In addition to this we will organize many other social events and collaborate with other clubs.

View Schedule for more details.

Coffee Lab and Roastery

Our final goal is fully dependent on the support of our members. Clemson Coffee Club would like to begin aquisition of equipment and space to start a Coffee Lab open to all students and staff. During our first year, members will have the opportunity to help us build a road map for this plan. Experiments on all types of coffee from filter, to espresso, and even latte art. This lab would have as its express goal to explore the preparation of coffee from roaster to cup.
